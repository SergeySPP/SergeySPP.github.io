Description

We fused card games and roguelikes together to make the best single player deckbuilder we could. Craft a unique deck, encounter bizarre creatures, discover relics of immense power, and Slay the Spire!

Features

Three core characters that each have their own unique set of cards. (With more planned) 200+ fully implemented cards. 50+ unique combat encounters. 100+ different items to be found. Procedurally generated levels. Tons of unlockables.
